02 — How we build
Stone & Steel Process
Stage 1 of 5
01
Site & Planning

Every great build starts with the right land.

We begin with a full site assessment — soil tests, grading surveys, utility locates, and zoning review. Most contractors skip this. We never do. An hour of planning saves weeks of rework.

02
Foundation

The base everything else depends on.

Reinforced concrete foundations engineered for Nevada's desert conditions — thermal expansion, seismic rating, and water table considered. We pour to Nevada state code plus 20%.

03
Framing & Structure

The skeleton rises.

Steel framing, structural lumber, and engineered floor systems. Our framing crews are the fastest in the valley — we've beaten every project schedule for 11 consecutive years. No exceptions.

04
MEP & Interior

Systems that last 50 years.

Mechanical, electrical, and plumbing rough-in. HVAC systems sized for Las Vegas summers — not the national average. Every install is inspected twice before drywall closes it in forever.

05
Finish & Handover

Keys in hand. On time.

Finish carpentry, tile, paint, fixtures, final inspections, and punch-list resolution — all coordinated by one dedicated project manager. You receive a fully documented build package at closeout.
