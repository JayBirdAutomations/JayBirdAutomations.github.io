FAQ

Common Questions

What exactly is an AI commercial? ▼
An AI commercial is a professionally produced video advertisement created using artificial intelligence — including AI-generated visuals, scenes, voiceovers, and music. The result is a broadcast-quality commercial that would traditionally cost $10,000–$100,000+ to produce, delivered in days at a fraction of the cost.
How long does it take to create an AI commercial? ▼
Most AI commercials are delivered within 3–7 business days from brief to final delivery, depending on complexity. Rush delivery is available within 48 hours for qualifying projects.
What are workflows and why do businesses need them? ▼
A workflow is a defined sequence of steps that completes a business task — for example, receiving a new lead, sending a welcome email, notifying your sales team, and logging it in your CRM. When these workflows are powered by AI, they run automatically without any human intervention, 24/7. AI-powered workflows eliminate manual repetition, reduce human error, and free your team to focus on high-value work. Jaybird Automations designs and builds custom AI workflows tailored to your exact business processes, so your company operates smarter and faster from day one.
What industries do you serve? ▼
We serve businesses across all industries including real estate, healthcare, retail, e-commerce, legal, financial services, restaurants, automotive, and more. AI automation and AI commercials apply to virtually any business vertical.
How does the booking and project process work? ▼
Start by submitting the contact form or booking a discovery call. Within 24 hours, we'll schedule a 30-minute strategy session, then present a custom proposal. Once approved, your project kicks off and you'll see the first deliverable within 48 hours.
What are AI agents and how can they help my business? ▼
AI agents are autonomous software systems powered by artificial intelligence that can independently think, plan, and execute multi-step tasks on your behalf — without constant human input. Unlike simple chatbots, AI agents can browse the web, analyze data, send emails, manage schedules, make decisions, and interact with other software tools. At Jaybird Automations, we build custom AI agents that work around the clock to handle customer outreach, lead follow-up, content creation, reporting, and much more — essentially giving your business a tireless digital employee.
