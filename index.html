08 — FAQ

Common questions.

What are AI agents and how can they help my business? ▼
AI agents are autonomous software systems that can independently think, plan, and execute multi-step tasks on your behalf — 24/7, without constant human input. They can browse the web, analyze data, send emails, manage schedules, and interact with other software. Jaybird Automations builds custom AI agents that handle customer outreach, lead follow-up, content creation, and reporting — essentially a tireless digital employee for your business.
What are workflows and why does my business need them? ▼
A workflow is a defined sequence of steps that completes a business task automatically — like receiving a lead, sending a welcome email, notifying your team, and logging it in your CRM. AI-powered workflows run 24/7 without human intervention, eliminating manual repetition, reducing errors, and freeing your team to focus on high-value work.
How long does it take to create an AI commercial? ▼
Most AI commercials are delivered within 3–7 business days from brief to final delivery. Rush delivery is available within 48 hours for qualifying projects. We deliver in all formats — TV, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn.
Do you work with law firms and corporations? ▼
Yes — law firms and corporations are among our primary clients. We build AI systems for case management automation, client intake workflows, AI-powered commercials, and document processing. Our Enterprise package is designed specifically for firms with complex, high-volume requirements.
