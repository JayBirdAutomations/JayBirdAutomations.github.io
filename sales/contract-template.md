## 2. Fees + Payment

- **Total fee:** $[AMOUNT]
- **Deposit:** 50% ($[AMOUNT]) due within 3 business days of signing. Work does not begin until deposit clears.
- **Balance:** 50% ($[AMOUNT]) due within 7 days of final delivery.

## 3. Timeline

- Project starts when: (a) this Agreement is signed, (b) deposit clears, (c) Client returns intake form and all required brand assets.
- Target delivery: **[DATE]**. This shifts day-for-day if Client delays review or asset delivery.
- Client has **5 business days** to review each deliverable. Silence past 5 days = approved.

## 4. Revisions

- Included rounds: **[1 / 2 / unlimited]**
- Additional rounds: $250 per round, billed after delivery.
- A "round" = one consolidated feedback batch. Feedback over multiple emails still counts as one round if delivered within 48 hours of each other.

## 5. Scope Changes

Any change outside Exhibit A requires a written change order with new fee + timeline, signed by both parties before work begins.

## 6. Ownership + License

- Upon final payment, Client owns the final deliverables produced under this Agreement (website files, chatbot knowledge base, workflow JSON, commercial videos, etc.).
- Provider retains the right to display the work in portfolio/case studies unless Client opts out in writing.
- Third-party tools (n8n, Cloudflare, Anthropic API, etc.) remain subject to their own licenses. Client is responsible for those accounts and fees.
- Provider's pre-existing templates, libraries, and code scaffolding are licensed to Client for use with the delivered project only — not for resale or reuse outside this project.

## 7. Client Responsibilities

- Provide all content, logos, photos, and access credentials on time.
- Guarantee they own the rights to everything they provide.
- Respond to review requests within 5 business days.
- Designate one point of contact for decisions.

## 8. Warranties + Disclaimers

- Provider warrants work will be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards.
- Provider makes NO guarantees regarding search engine rankings, traffic, leads, conversion rates, or revenue.
- Software/services provided "as is" with a 30-day bug-fix guarantee after final delivery. Bugs = deliverable doesn't perform as specified in Exhibit A. New features requested after delivery are NOT bugs.

## 9. Confidentiality

Each party will keep the other's non-public business information confidential. Obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

## 10. Indemnity

Client indemnifies Provider against claims arising from content Client provides (copyright, trademark, likeness, defamation, etc.). Provider indemnifies Client against claims arising from Provider's negligent work.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Neither party's liability under this Agreement exceeds the total fees paid in the preceding 6 months. Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

## 12. Termination

- Either party may terminate with 30 days' written notice.
- If Client terminates after work begins, deposit is non-refundable and Client pays for work completed to date.
- If Provider terminates early without cause, Client receives a pro-rata refund.

## 13. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by Nevada law. Disputes resolved in Clark County, Nevada courts.

## 14. Entire Agreement

This document + Exhibit A + any signed change orders constitute the full agreement between the parties. Supersedes all prior discussions.
