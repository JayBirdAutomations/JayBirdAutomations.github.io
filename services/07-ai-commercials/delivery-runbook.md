# 07 — AI Commercials

**Price:** $1,499 per 15–30 sec spot · **Timeline:** 3–7 days · **Deposit:** $750 (50%)

## Tech stack

- **Freepik Pikaso Spaces** — start + end image generation
- **Kling** (via Freepik) — image-to-video (7 sec at 24fps, 1:1 or 16:9)
- **ElevenLabs** — AI voiceover
- **DaVinci Resolve** (free) or Premiere — final assembly + color + text
- **Suno** (optional) — custom background music

## Intake questions

1. **Goal:** Brand awareness? Promotion of a specific offer? Hiring? Testimonial-style?
2. **Where will it run?** TV? YouTube pre-roll? Instagram/TikTok? All?
3. **Length:** 15s (Insta/TikTok), 30s (TV/YouTube), 60s (longform)?
4. **Must-have elements:** Logo? Tagline? Phone number? URL? CTA?
5. **Brand visuals:** Logo file, primary/secondary colors, any existing footage or photos to match
6. **Voice:** Male/female? Age range? Tone (authoritative, friendly, excited)?
7. **Examples they love:** Send me 2–3 commercials they want to feel like

## Delivery steps

1. **Script** — draft 2 versions. Client picks one. Max ~75 words for 30s.
2. **Storyboard** — break script into 3–6 shots. Write start + end prompt for each.
3. **Generate images** in Freepik Pikaso:
   - Set a SPACE for consistent characters/setting
   - Generate start + end image per shot
   - Iterate until you love them — cheap to redo
4. **Generate video** — image-to-video in Kling for each shot. Usually 7s clips.
5. **Voiceover** in ElevenLabs — pick voice, generate, regenerate lines until clean.
6. **Music** — royalty-free from Pixabay/Uppbeat OR Suno custom.
7. **Assemble** in DaVinci/Premiere — cut clips to rhythm, add voiceover, music bed, text overlays (logo, CTA), color correct for consistency.
8. **Export** — deliver in 3 formats:
   - 1080p 16:9 (YouTube/TV)
   - 1080p 9:16 (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
   - 1080p 1:1 (Instagram feed)
9. **1 revision round** — batch all feedback, don't do one-at-a-time.
10. **Final delivery** — signed MP4 files + Google Drive link, saved to `clients/<slug>/04-deliverables/commercial-vN.mp4`.
